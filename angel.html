• ☆ About the Artist ☆

    Klemen Price is a 16-year-old original character & fiction based-artist from Virginia. They currently write "Where The Water Didn't Reach," a short story set in 1996 Sylva, North Carolina, which explores religion and violation through the eyes of three ill-fated teenagers.

    Klemen graduated from Hermitage High School on May 28th, 2025, and is an incoming student of Columbia College Chicago, where they'll be studying animation and game art.
